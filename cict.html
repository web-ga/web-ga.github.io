COLLEGE OF INFORMATION AND COMMUNICATIONS TECHNOLOGY


HISTORY


In 1993, Holy Angel University offered its first 2-year Associate program in Computer Technology, By School Year 1996-1997, the CHED granted permit to the University to offer Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. In 2003, the Department was led under the leadership of Mr. Joseph A. Esquivel as chairperson. The Department became a college in School Year 2005-2006, under the supervision of Mr. Joseph A. Esquivel, which was then named College of Information and Communications Technology


MISSION


To offer quality education and produce technically-competent and globally competitive professionals who are self-reliant, morally and spiritually upright, adaptive to current and emerging technologies, and who contribute to community building.


VISION


A Center of Excellence in ICT education through relevant curricular programs implemented by highly competent individuals who engage in research and countryside development and supported by the industry and state-of-the-art facilities.



Course Offerings


Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
With Areas of Specialization in:

  • Web Development
  • Network Administration
  • Animation

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science



Curricula

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology - Network Administration

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology - Web Development

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
